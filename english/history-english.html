History

1 year ago more or less a good friend and me started deejaying, that obvioulsy make us start downloading a lot of music for our sets! We could just download and save the songs on our different styles folders but the result after entering any software and looking at the music bookstore is just... Disgusting.

Unplaced photos, incorrect photos or photos with bad dimensions, unnecessary metadata that some songs have and others don't, name and artist misspelled or not even written at all, incorrect genres and a long etc. If you are a neat freak (like me) this program will help you to fix this annoying problem!
